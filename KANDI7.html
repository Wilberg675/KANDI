Growing Demand

With nearly 1.2 million EVs on the road in the U.S. in 2019, and more hitting the market very soon, it is crucial for dealers to seriously consider the future of the industry. At Kandi, we are making EVs accessible for Americans with pricing starting at just $12,499.



Profitability



Minimal inventory requirements and auto-replenishment model By partnering with Kandi, you are required to have a minimal number of vehicles on your lot compared to most agreements with manufacturers. Each time you sell a car, we will automatically replenish your stock. This model reduces initial investment and financial risk.



Complete price transparency and no-haggle price policy Keeping the price the same shortens the customer sales process and reduces the risk of losing a customer to a competing Kandi dealer, all while guaranteeing a consistent amount of profit for each sale.



Kandi’s virtual customer experience

Kandi invests heavily on the frontend to increase the number of qualified leads and maximize efficiency.



Exposure



Through Kandi’s direct-to-consumer experience, the customer acquisition process dramatically changes for dealers. A substantial portion of the sales process will be completed online through a consumer-facing website and fueled by highly-targeted digital marketing. This drives more qualified customers with an intent to purchase to visit their local dealership for a test drive. From there, the dealers finalize the sales process by assisting with the test drive, providing details about financing and overseeing vehicle trade-ins.
